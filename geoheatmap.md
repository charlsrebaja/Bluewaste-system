Act as a professional Frontend Developer and GIS UI/UX Designer and implement a Geographic Heatmap in the BlueWaste map dashboard.

The heatmap should visualize the density of waste reports based on their geographic coordinates, allowing users to easily identify waste hotspots or areas with high report activity.

Requirements:

Generate the heatmap using report latitude and longitude data.

Areas with more reports should appear more intense (warmer colors).

Ensure the heatmap loads automatically when the map opens.

Allow the heatmap to update dynamically when new reports are added.

Maintain smooth performance even with many reports.

The design should remain clean, modern, and easy to understand for LGU monitoring.

The goal is to help the LGU quickly identify waste problem areas and prioritize cleanup efforts.
